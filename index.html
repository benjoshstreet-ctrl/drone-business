From Lemonade Stand to the Sky 🍋 🚁

We’re Ben and Josh, two brothers who love technology, flight, and building things together.

Last summer, we set a goal: run a lemonade stand until we earned enough money to buy our first professional drone. After weeks of hard work, saving every dollar, and learning the ropes, we officially launched our drone business with a DJI Mini 4 Pro.

Today, we take crisp, high-resolution aerial photos and video for real estate listings, local businesses, and community events. Every job we complete helps us gain real-world experience and goes directly toward saving for our college funds.
