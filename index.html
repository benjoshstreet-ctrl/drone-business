Our Story

We didn't just ask our parents for a toy drone. We ran a crazy-successful neighborhood lemonade stand, pooled every single cent of our savings together to buy a professional DJI Mini 4 Pro, and launched a real business.

My 9-year-old brother, Josh, and I, Ben, started this drone business to get a head start on saving for our college funds.

Meet the Crew

Ben • Age 11

Ben: Lead Pilot & Photographer

My passion for this started with a love for math and practicing coding. Once I was old enough, I joined the school drone team. In my very first year of flying, I earned 4 Gold Trophies at the REC Foundation Aerial Drone Competition and made it all the way to regionals in California.

Josh • Age 9

Josh: Operations & Editing

I knew I wanted to turn this idea into a real company, so I asked my brother, Josh, to tag along with me. To make the business a reality, we pooled our savings from a neighborhood lemonade stand to buy a professional DJI Mini 4 Pro.
