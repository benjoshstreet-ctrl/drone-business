Meet the Crew

How we teamed up to build this brother-owned drone business from the ground up.

Ben • Age 11

Ben: Lead Pilot & Photographer

I am the pilot behind our aerial views. When Josh and I decided to buy a professional drone from our savings, I wanted to make sure we're the safest choice out there. My edge comes from winning 4 Gold Trophies at the REC Foundation Aerial Drone Competition and making it all the way to the regional finals. That tournament-precision training taught me how to handle safe flight paths, so I know exactly how to keep our aircraft rock-steady and smoothly frame your property lines without any close calls.

Josh • Age 9

Josh: Operations & Editing

I run our ground command station and client communication grids. After counting up our summer lemonade stand savings box together, my job was to design our booking schedules and manage our raw photo media assets. I handle our client email folders and double-check all of our drone battery and safety landing gear before every flight so Ben can focus entirely on executing your shoot.
