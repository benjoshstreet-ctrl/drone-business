Our Story

We didn't just ask our parents for a toy drone. We spent months running a neighborhood lemonade stand, pooled every single cent of our savings together, and bought a professional DJI Mini 4 Pro to launch a real business.

My 9-year-old brother, Josh, and I started The Drone Brothers to get a serious head start on saving for our future college funds. We combine competitive drone navigation experience with high-quality cameras to deliver clean, professional media for our neighborhood and local real estate listings.

Meet the Crew

Ben • Age 11

Ben: Lead Pilot & Photographer

I fell in love with flying after practicing precision math layout systems and robotics tracks. Last year, I joined the school competitive drone team and won 4 Gold Trophies at the REC Foundation Aerial Drone Competition, making it all the way to the regional championship finals in California.

That specialized training taught me how to keep the drone perfectly steady, navigate complex property lines safely, and capture crisp, level overhead angles that make houses and events look incredibly clean.

Josh • Age 9

Josh: Operations & Editing

While Ben is handling the flight paths up in the air, I command our entire operational setup down on the ground. I take care of downloading the raw 4K memory files, organizing our email folders, and tracking our booking calendar matrix so our clients get their media right on time.

I also help run our equipment safety checks before every single launch so our gear stays pristine. When you send an email or request a flight quote, you'll be talking directly to me!
