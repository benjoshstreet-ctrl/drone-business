Our Story

We didn't just ask our parents for a toy drone. We ran a crazy-successful neighborhood lemonade stand, pooled every single cent of our savings together to buy a professional DJI Mini 4 Pro, and launched a real business.

My 9-year-old brother, Josh, and I, Ben, started this drone business to get a head start on saving for our college funds.

Meet the Crew

Ben • Age 11

Ben: Lead Pilot & Photographer

My passion for this started with a love for math and practicing coding. Once I was old enough, I joined the school drone team. In my very first year of flying, I earned 4 Gold Trophies at the REC Foundation Aerial Drone Competition and made it all the way to regionals in California.

That training taught me how to fly with total precision—keeping the aircraft perfectly steady, navigating safely through tight gaps, and capturing aerial angles that make every event and project look its absolute best.

Josh • Age 9

Josh: Operations & Editing

I knew I wanted to turn this idea into a real company, so I asked my brother, Josh, to tag along with me, and he was incredibly happy to join the team. To make the business a reality, we pooled our savings from a neighborhood lemonade stand to buy a professional DJI Mini 4 Pro.

While I focus on capturing perfect shots, smooth videos, and stunning aerial footage, Josh commands our digital operations on the ground. He transforms our raw files into polished media, handles our email replies, and keeps our schedule on track.
